Cyber Security and Internet Safety

Protecting your digital world

Introduction into IT Awareness

In today’s digital world, cyber security threats pose significant risks to businesses across all industries. As organizations rely more on technology, it is important that employees know how to protect the company and themselves from risks. This information is designed to inform staff members on how to understand the main cybersecurity threats that the company faces and provide clear steps on how to prevent, identify and deal with these security issues. This website will focus on the key cybersecurity risks that could harm the company’s data, systems and operations. It will give you as employees practical advice on how to recognize and avoid cyberattacks. Additionally, this report will also discuss problems that might arise when handling cybersecurity issues and will suggest ways on how to deal with them. Through rising awareness and taking action, this program aims to empower every staff member to play a part in keeping the company safe and secure.
